Projects

Logistics Management

In our Logistics Management project, we aimed to optimize transportation and distribution processes within our supply chain to enhance efficiency and reduce costs. By analyzing key metrics like lead times and inventory turnover, we identified bottlenecks and proposed operational improvements, including the implementation of advanced technologies and sustainable practices. The project aimed to create a more agile, responsive, and sustainable logistics network capable of meeting our business needs.

Spotify Web App

In our Spotify Web App project, we aimed to create a seamless music streaming experience directly from the web browser. Our goal was to provide users easy access to Spotify's vast library while offering an intuitive interface for discovering, organizing, and enjoying their favorite songs and playlists. With meticulous design, robust search functionality, and responsive playback controls, users could enjoy their music exactly as desired. Our focus on responsive design ensured seamless functionality across devices. Overall, our project aimed to deliver a convenient and immersive music streaming experience, accessible anytime, anywhere, from the web browser.
